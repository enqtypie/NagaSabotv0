What is NagaSabot?

NagaSabot is a cutting-edge platform that uses artificial intelligence to perform lip-reading in the Bikol-Naga dialect. It combines CNN and LSTM technologies to help people with hearing disabilities better understand and communicate in their local language.

üíª

How does it work?

Our system processes video input through advanced neural networks. The CNN analyzes visual features of lip movements, while the LSTM network interprets these movements as temporal sequences. This combination allows for accurate real-time translation of lip movements to text.

üîí

Is my data secure?

Yes! We prioritize your privacy and security. All video processing happens locally on your device, and we don't store any of your recordings. Our camera and storage access permissions are used solely for the lip-reading functionality.

What devices are supported?

NagaSabot works on most modern devices with a camera, including smartphones, tablets, and computers. For optimal performance, we recommend using a device with a good quality front-facing camera and updated browser.

Can I use it offline?

Currently, NagaSabot requires an internet connection for initial loading, but once loaded, the core lip-reading features work offline. We're working on a fully offline-capable version for future releases.

How accurate is it?

Our system doesnt have a model yet but we plan to achieve an average accuracy rate of 85% in controlled environments. Factors like lighting, camera quality, and clear pronunciation can affect accuracy. We're constantly improving our algorithms for better performance.
