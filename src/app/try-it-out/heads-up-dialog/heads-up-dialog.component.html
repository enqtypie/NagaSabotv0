Important Information

Development Phase Notice

This lip reading model is currently in development and is optimized for normal lip formations. Accuracy may vary depending on various factors.

Tips for Better Accuracy:

  • Ensure good lighting on your face
  • Keep your head straight (avoid tilting)
  • Position your face within the guide box
  • Speak clearly and at a normal pace
  • Avoid excessive head movement
  • Maintain a neutral facial expression

Best Recording Practices:

  • Record in a well-lit environment
  • Avoid backlighting
  • Keep a stable head position
  • Face the camera directly
  • Stay within 2-3 feet from the camera
